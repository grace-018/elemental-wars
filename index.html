HOW TO PLAY

  • The objective of the game is to earn points by winning battles with the opponent.
  • Each player will receive a hand of six Elementtal cards, each can be used in the battle TWICE
  • Elemental Cards are:
  • To start a battle, players will select one card from their hand to use.
  • Click the Fight button to initiate the battle.
  • A player earns a point if their selected element defeats the opponent's selected element based on the following rules:
    • Wind defeats Water
    • Water defeats Fire
    • Fire defeats Earth
    • Earth defeats Wind
    • Light defeats Dark, but against any other element, it results in a draw
    • Dark defeats all elements except Light
  • The game ends when all six cards have been played, and the player with the most points at the end wins the game.
