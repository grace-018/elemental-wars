    HOW TO PLAY

    • The objective is to earn points by winning elemental battles against the computer.
    • Before starting, choose your settings on the home screen:
      • Difficulty — Easy: computer picks randomly. Hard: computer counters your last played element.
      • Game Mode — Quick: first to 4 pts. Classic: first to 6 pts. Marathon: first to 8 pts.
    • Each player receives a hand of six Elemental cards. Each card can be played TWICE — two gold stars (★★) below each card show remaining uses. One star dims (★☆) after the first use and the card disappears after the second.
    • Elemental Cards are:
    • Select a card from your hand, then click the Fight button to battle the computer's hidden card.
    • A player earns 1 point when their element defeats the opponent's, based on these rules:
      • Wind defeats Water
      • Water defeats Fire
      • Fire defeats Earth
      • Earth defeats Wind
      • Light defeats Dark, but draws against all other elements
      • Dark defeats all elements except Light
    • Win Streak Bonus — win 3 battles in a row to earn +1 Bonus Point! The streak resets on a loss or tie. The computer can earn a streak bonus too, so watch out!
    • The game ends when a player reaches the point target, or when all 12 battles (6 cards × 2 uses) are played — whichever comes first. If all cards are exhausted, the player with the most points wins.
